# Counterbalance — Rationale

Plugin-specific mechanics. Background thinking — AI slop, voice as yours, agile for writing, the ADHD note-overflow problem — lives in [`writings/post-seeds.md`](../writings/post-seeds.md).

## Mechanisms

- **Ghost drafting** — generate drafts against text based on how you'd say it. The uncanny valley forces you to correct, which helps you understand what you're trying to say from bullet points. The engine tries to connect your notes linearly.
- **Reviewers** — cold-reader reviewer agents that help you find things in a draft.
- **Voice discovery** — tools to both prompt and discover how you want to write, along with granular controls.
